# REX LinkedIn Expert

Build your REX Expert LinkedIn thought leadership presence aligned with HqO's REX methodology. This plugin helps HqO employees go from blank page to confident LinkedIn poster with a personalized voice, content strategy, and ongoing coaching.

## Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/rex-expert-setup` | Define your unique niche, voice, and content strategy. Interactive intake that analyzes your writing style and builds a complete LinkedIn playbook tailored to you. |
| `/rex-post` | Draft a LinkedIn post in your voice. Bring a topic, data point, or article URL and get a ready-to-publish post with proper hashtags, stats, and audience framing. Use weekly. |
| `/rex-review` | Review what's working, refine your niche, and get fresh content ideas. Your positioning grows with you. Use monthly. |

## Skills

| Skill | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `rex-linkedin-expert` | Core REX methodology knowledge including the 5:3:2 content framework, voice analysis protocol, Prior Career Fade Strategy, HqO leadership cross-linking guidance, hashtag strategy, and style/tone rules. |

## How to Use

1. Run `/rex-expert-setup` and walk through the intake questions
2. Paste 2-3 writing samples (Slack messages, emails, anything that sounds like you)
3. Get your complete playbook with niche definition, sample posts, and content calendar
4. Each week, use `/rex-post` with a topic to draft a new post
5. Each month, use `/rex-review` to refine your positioning and get fresh ideas

## Tips

Your writing samples are the single most valuable input. Even casual examples produce dramatically more authentic posts than any description of your style.
