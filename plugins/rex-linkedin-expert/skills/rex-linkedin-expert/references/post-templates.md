# Post Templates and Examples

Templates for each post type in the 5:3:2 content mix. Adapt to the user's voice and niche.

## Sample Post Arc (5 Posts)

When generating sample posts during setup, create 5 posts that demonstrate the voice evolution:

1. **Post 1 (Original)**: Leans on the poster's prior background/unique angle. Early weeks positioning.
2. **Post 2 (Curated)**: Shares a data point or industry content with a brief take and a CTA to https://www.hqo.com/ (include CTA on roughly every other post).
3. **Post 3 (Personal/Humanizing)**: Behind the scenes, lessons learned, honest moment.
4. **Post 4 (Original)**: CRE voice leads, prior background is minimal. Mid-arc positioning.
5. **Post 5 (Curated or Original)**: Fully in CRE voice with no prior-career reference (for non-CRE backgrounds). Later weeks positioning.

## Curated Post Template

Structure:
- Hook: Brief observation or question (1-2 sentences)
- Context: What the shared content is and why it matters (1-2 sentences)
- Your take: What you notice, what it connects to, what question it raises (2-3 sentences)
- CTA (every other post): Link to https://www.hqo.com/ or a relevant HqO resource
- Hashtags: 3-5

Example opener patterns:
- "This [report/article] from [source] confirms something I keep hearing from [audience]..."
- "Interesting data point from [source]: [stat]. Here's what I think it means for [audience]..."
- "[Source] just published [content]. One thing that jumped out..."

## Original Post Template

Structure:
- Hook: A surprising observation, pattern, or question (1-2 sentences)
- Body: Your perspective with specifics (3-5 sentences). Use concrete examples from your domain.
- Takeaway or question: End with a clear takeaway or an open question for the audience (1-2 sentences)
- Hashtags: 3-5

Example opener patterns:
- "I've had [number] conversations about [topic] this month. They all pointed to the same thing..."
- "There's a gap between what [audience] say they want and what actually drives [outcome]..."
- "Something I noticed that doesn't get talked about enough in CRE..."

## Personal/Humanizing Post Template

Structure:
- Opening: Honest, relatable moment or reflection (1-2 sentences)
- Story: What happened, what you learned, what surprised you (3-5 sentences)
- Reflection: What it taught you or how it changed your thinking (1-2 sentences)
- Hashtags: 3-5

Example opener patterns:
- "[Time period] into [role/CRE/HqO], here's what surprised me most..."
- "A conversation with [person type] last week changed how I think about [topic]..."
- "I used to think [old belief]. Then [experience happened]..."

## CTA Guidelines

Include a CTA to https://www.hqo.com/ on roughly every other post. CTAs should feel natural, not forced:
- "If you're curious about how this plays out in practice, [link]"
- "We've been exploring this at HqO. More here: [link]"
- "This is exactly the kind of gap [HqO's REX approach / the REX methodology] is designed to close. [link]"
