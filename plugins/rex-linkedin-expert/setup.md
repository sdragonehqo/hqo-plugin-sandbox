# Setup: rex-linkedin-expert

## Requirements

- No MCP connectors required
- No external credentials or API keys required
- No dependencies on other plugins

## First-Run Steps

1. Install the plugin from the HqO marketplace — no additional configuration is needed before first use.
2. On first use, the skill will guide you through a niche intake questionnaire (sourced from `references/intake-questions.md`) to build your LinkedIn playbook. Have the following ready:
   - Your name, title, and department at HqO
   - A description of your primary audience (e.g., property managers, asset managers, CRE leasing teams)
   - 2–4 sentences about your career background before HqO, or your CRE domain focus if you've always been in CRE
   - 2–3 writing samples that sound like you at your best (LinkedIn posts, client emails, Slack messages, internal notes — anything unpolished is fine)
   - Your realistic weekly posting frequency on LinkedIn
3. The skill will analyze your writing samples to define your voice and niche, then produce a LinkedIn content playbook aligned with HqO's REX methodology.

## Verification

Invoke the skill by asking about "REX Expert positioning", "LinkedIn thought leadership for HqO", "my REX niche", or "help me write a LinkedIn post." The skill should respond with context-appropriate guidance based on the REX methodology, the 5:3:2 content framework, and (once intake is complete) your personal niche and voice profile.

## Notes

- No scheduled tasks or background automation — this plugin is fully on-demand.
- The skill uses a 5:3:2 content framework: 5 curated posts, 3 original posts, 2 personal/humanizing posts per 10 posts.
- For employees from outside CRE, the skill applies a "prior career fade strategy" — prior-career references phase out over the first 8+ weeks as the CRE voice strengthens.
- All data points and statistics used in post drafts are flagged for verification before use. The skill will not include an unverified stat without noting it.
- The skill references HqO leadership voices (Chase Garbarino, Greg Gomer, Shelly Just, Tom Herbert) for cross-linking guidance — no connection to their accounts is required.
- Post drafts use the user's analyzed voice and never include em dashes.
