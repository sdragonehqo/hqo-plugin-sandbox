Help the user review and evolve their REX Expert positioning. Use monthly.

## Step 1: Load Knowledge

Read the skill and reference files:
- `${CLAUDE_PLUGIN_ROOT}/skills/rex-linkedin-expert/SKILL.md`
- `${CLAUDE_PLUGIN_ROOT}/skills/rex-linkedin-expert/references/post-templates.md`
- `${CLAUDE_PLUGIN_ROOT}/skills/rex-linkedin-expert/references/hashtag-library.md`

## Step 2: Gather Context

Ask the user:
1. **What posts have you published since your last review?** (Ask them to paste or describe their recent posts, or share which ones performed well.)
2. **What's been resonating?** Any posts that got more engagement than usual? Any topics that sparked conversations?
3. **What felt off?** Any posts that fell flat or didn't feel authentic?
4. **What's new in your world?** Any shifts in your role, audience, or what you're noticing in the market?

## Step 3: Analyze Performance

If the user shares posts they've published:
- Identify patterns in what worked (topic, format, length, hook style, time of posting)
- Note what didn't resonate and hypothesize why
- Compare their posted content against their established voice profile and niche

## Step 4: Evolve Positioning

Based on the review:
- Assess whether the current niche is still right or needs refinement
- Suggest whether it's time to expand into adjacent topics
- Recommend any voice or tone adjustments
- Update the Prior Career Fade Strategy timeline if applicable (are prior-career references fading appropriately?)

## Step 5: Generate Fresh Ideas

Provide:
- **5 post topic ideas** tailored to current trends and the user's niche. Use WebSearch to find recent CRE industry news, Leesman data, or relevant reports to inform these suggestions.
- **2 cross-linking opportunities**: Specific recent posts from Chase, Greg, Shelly, or Tom that the user could engage with from their angle.
- **1 content experiment**: A slightly different format or angle to try (e.g., a question-only post, a contrarian take, a "what I got wrong" post).

## Step 6: Set Next Month's Plan

Summarize:
- Updated niche positioning (if changed)
- Recommended posting rhythm for the coming month
- Top 3 topics to prioritize
- Any adjustments to hashtag strategy or audience framing
